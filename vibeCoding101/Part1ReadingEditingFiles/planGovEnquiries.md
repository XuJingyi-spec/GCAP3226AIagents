
# Government Enquiry Plan: Flu Vaccination Project

## 1. Data to be Collected
1. Vaccination rates, infection rates, and hospitalization rates by age group and population segment
2. Self-paid vaccination data for groups not covered by subsidies
3. Influenza infection and hospitalization data by age and region
4. Information on nasal spray vaccine subsidies and effectiveness
5. School-level vaccination and infection data
6. Policy documents, data collection standards, and evaluation methods
7. Weather data related to influenza infection

## 2. Recommended Departments to Contact
- Department of Health: Vaccination, infection, hospitalization, and policy data
- Education Bureau: School-level vaccination and infection data
- Hospital Authority: Hospitalization and severe case data
- Hong Kong Observatory / Meteorological Department: Weather and influenza-related data

## 3. Suggested Enquiry Email Content
### Department of Health
- Request vaccination, infection, and hospitalization rates by age group and population segment
- Request self-paid vaccination data for non-subsidized groups
- Request influenza infection and hospitalization data by age and region
- Request information on nasal spray vaccine subsidies and effectiveness
- Request policy documents, data collection standards, and evaluation methods

### Education Bureau
- Request school-level vaccination and infection data

### Hospital Authority
- Request influenza hospitalization and severe case data by age and region

### Hong Kong Observatory / Meteorological Department
- Request weather data related to influenza infection

## 4. Next Steps
1. Organize specific enquiry questions and draft email templates
2. Identify the responsible department and contact person for each data item
3. Assign tasks according to the project plan and send enquiry emails
4. Follow up on data responses and supplement analysis
